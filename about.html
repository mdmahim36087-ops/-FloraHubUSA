Who We Are

Our Brand Story

Established in the heart of Oregon, FloraHub USA is dedicated to bridging the gap between professional nursery cultivation and home plant enthusiasts.

Cultivating with Care Since 2018

What started as a modest 500 sq ft glasshouse in Portland has grown into a premium multi-acre facility nurturing thousands of species. We select and cultivate cultivars suited for the local indoor environments and varied US landscape hardiness zones.

Our nursery philosophy rejects massive commercial speed-growing in favor of slow, organic root development, ensuring each specimen reaches your hands with robust root systems and acclimated cells capable of immediately handling domestic environments.

"Our mission is simple: to make shopping for plants as joyful and stress-free as growing them. We pack each pot by hand, because a living plant is not just merchandise; it is a piece of art."

Our Core Values

Sustainable Cultivation

We utilize capture-and-reuse drip irrigation to reduce water waste and fertilize using organic compost formulas rather than synthetic runoff chemicals.

Eco-Friendly Wrap

Our shipping sleeves are 100% recyclable, made from organic cellulose fibres that conserve heat during transport without building up plastic landfill waste.

Grower Support

We provide a lifetime plant care hotline. Whether you have problems with yellowing leaves or root rots, our nursery team is ready to advise.
